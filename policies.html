Financing Policies

We offer flexible payment options for larger orders. Here are the details:

  • We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and cash.
  • Payment plans are available for orders over $500.
  • Contact us to discuss financing arrangements and terms.

Delivery Policies

Enjoy fresh breakfast and market goods delivered to your door:

  • Local delivery available within a 5-mile radius.
  • Flat delivery fee of $5 for orders under $50.
  • Free delivery for catering orders over $200.

Return Policies

Your satisfaction is our priority. Here's how we handle returns:

  • No returns on perishable items. Contact us within 24 hours for quality issues.
  • Returns accepted on non-perishable items within two weeks with receipt.
  • Refunds for catering orders considered on a case-by-case basis.
