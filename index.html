Our History

Jim’s Market was established in 1954 by a local family with a passion for great food and a dedication to serving their community. Originally a small corner store specializing in fresh produce, pantry staples, and grab-and-go sandwiches, the market quickly became a staple in its neighborhood. Over the years, we’ve grown to offer a wide variety of products, including our famous “B-Boys” breakfast sandwiches, which have become a favorite for locals and visitors alike. Our growth hasn’t just been about expanding our offerings,it’s also been about giving back. From the early days, we’ve partnered with local charities to support food drives and provide meals to families in need. Today, we continue that tradition by donating a portion of our profits to community organizations and supporting events that bring people together. Jim’s Market is proud to have grown alongside Wakefield, staying true to our roots while embracing opportunities to serve our customers better every day.

Why us?

With nearly 50 years of experience serving the Medford community, we’ve perfected the balance between quality, convenience, and care. Our products are thoughtfully selected, from the freshest ingredients in our meals to everyday grocery essentials. At Jim’s Market, we care about more than just great food, we care about helping people. That’s why we’re committed to fighting hunger, both in our neighborhood and beyond. We regularly donate meals to local families in need and work with charities to make sure no one in our community goes hungry. By partnering with organizations focused on ending world hunger, we’re helping to make a difference globally, too. When you shop at Jim’s Market, you’re not just buying groceries, you’re helping us support these important causes. Together, we’re making our neighborhood stronger and working toward a future where everyone has enough to eat.
